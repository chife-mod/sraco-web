### 🔒 Advisor note (why V2, not V1)

The client objected to **timeline (90 days), not price** — V1's `$14.6–21.3k` was accepted silently.
Timeline was solved separately (see below), so switching to V2 leaves **~$1,600–2,100 on the table**
the client never asked for. **Oleg chose V2 knowingly** (2026-06-11) — felt the sum was already large
for the scope and preferred the lower, faster-to-close number. Decision recorded, not a default.

## Key framing decisions baked into the live offer (V2)

- **Timeline in weeks, not days.** Headline shows `6–8 weeks` (calendar, parallel streams) instead of
  raw "90 working days" — answers the client's actual objection without dropping price. Phased section
  (Discovery+Wireframes ∥ Visual Design ∥ Company pages + RTL → 6–8 weeks) + a dependency clause
  ("timely content & consolidated feedback; late content / scope shifts the timeline").
- **Price flexibility note:** "final estimate confirmed once the sitemap and wireframes are approved."
- **Arabic-first, designed in English** — stated in the Bilingual/RTL block caption.
- **Design System** is its own block (after Homepage), not part of Design Concept — it's production.
- **Rounding:** headline floored to clean $100; **detailed table stays exact and self-summing** (line
  items rounded to whole dollars, total = their sum — no phantom $1 mismatch).
- Recommended process: **Concept directions and Wireframes run in parallel** at the start; full
  page-by-page visual design begins only once concept is chosen and wireframes approved.
