Preliminary estimate — design + animation only. Hours cover both languages (English + Arabic-first / RTL) and all breakpoints (desktop, tablet, mobile). The min–max range depends on the complexity of each block / section and is finalised after the page and block structure is approved. Working days are production only — they exclude possible management, comments or rework. Front-end build, CMS, ERP / CRM integrations, hosting, security and VAPT are out of design scope and handled with SRACO ITC (shown for context, not included in the figure). Video generation is not included — third-party generation / render services may be billed separately, with the amount known after the concept.
